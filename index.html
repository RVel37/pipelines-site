    • Mixed Preprocessing

    This pipeline processes ALL samples together: genome, exome, research and targeted NGS samples. Samples are processed on 25B Illumina flowcells on the Novaseq X Plus. This preprocessing pipeline takes the raw BCL outputs and converts them to FASTQs, ready for downstream processing by the EG and tREX pipelines. MultiQC reports are also produced.
